## What creators pay in

EarnFi job budgets are priced in **USDC on Solana**. When you fund work, you’ll see a clear checkout-style summary in the creator flow.

## Platform fee

When you create or **expand** a job (add more participants), the app shows:

- **Participant rewards** × **slots** (subtotal paid to earners)  
- **Platform fee** as a percentage of that subtotal (shown live in the job builder)  
- **Total** charged to your creator wallet balance  

The fee funds platform operations and ecosystem features (for example token mechanics surfaced elsewhere in the product).

## Job creation flow (high level)

1. Choose a **job type** (social, video, manual, or contest).  
2. Set **reward per participant**, **number of participants**, and any **requirements** (watch %, lock duration, manual instructions, etc.).  
3. Preview **content** when the type supports it (for example social targets or video metadata).  
4. Review the **cost summary** (rewards + platform fee).  
5. **Fund** from your connected wallet balance.  

Manual jobs and contests may add extra fields (instructions, attachments guidance, winner settings). The live form is always authoritative.

## Lock periods (social jobs)

For many **social** job types, you choose how long rewards stay **locked** after a successful verification. **Manual** jobs and **video** jobs use a **zero-hour lock** in the standard configuration—the creator UI explains what applies to the type you picked.

**Follow** jobs measure follow duration differently (hours/days family). Use the limits shown in the job builder.

## Manual verification and your responsibility

Manual jobs create a **review queue** for you:

- Approve good submissions quickly.  
- Reject with a **clear reason** when proof does not match the instructions.  

Each manual job is configured with an **automatic finalization window**: if you do not approve or reject in time, the platform may **finalize pending submissions automatically** so participants are not stuck indefinitely. The app communicates timing through notifications and verification screens.

If you reject someone, they may have a **short dispute window** to respond with evidence—treat rejections as factual and specific.

## Contests: winners and fairness

When you create a contest you choose:

- **How many winners**  
- **Winner selection**: **Manual** (you pick) or **Random**  
- **Prize mode**: **tiered** prizes or **equal** prizes  

If you choose **manual selection**, you must pick winners within the **time window shown in the contest setup** after the contest deadline. If you do not, the platform **selects winners automatically** so the contest can close and payouts can proceed.

## Video jobs: watch percentage and minimum reward

Video jobs require a **minimum watch percentage** (configured per job). Participants must watch at least that much of the video to complete.

In the creator builder, **longer videos** and **higher watch requirements** can increase the **suggested minimum reward** for the job. The UI guides you so the offer stays fair for the time and attention required.

## Disputes

Both creators and earners can end up in **disputes** when outcomes are contested. Keep communication in the dispute thread professional, cite the original job requirements, and upload **clear image evidence** when attachments are needed.

## Automation at scale

- **Application automation** (API keys, your own services, agency tools): use the **[Work API](https://app.earnfi.fun/work-api)** explorer—this is the developer integration layer.  
- **Autonomous agents / agentic commerce**: use the separate **[Agent API](https://app.earnfi.fun/agent-api)** explorer—a different product surface; it does not build on Work API keys.
