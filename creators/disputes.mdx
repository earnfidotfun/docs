## What disputes are

Disputes are used when there’s disagreement about a job outcome (for example, proof quality or requirements).

## Where to manage disputes

Open **Creator hub → Disputes** to:

- View disputes for your jobs
- Respond and provide context
- Track unread messages and status changes

## Best practices

- Reference the original job requirements in your responses.
- Keep proof links/screenshots organized so review is fast.
