## Jobs page

In the Creator hub, **Jobs** lists the jobs you created. Use it to:

- Review job details and progress
- Monitor completions and open slots
- Make updates as needed

## Tips for higher-quality submissions

- Write requirements that are easy to verify.
- Specify what counts as valid proof (link, screenshot, or both).
- Call out common invalid cases to reduce back-and-forth.
