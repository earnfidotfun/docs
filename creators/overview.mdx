## What you can do

The Creator hub is where you manage everything related to jobs you create:

- Create jobs
- Track performance over time (by period)
- Review activity and progress
- Handle disputes and verifications
- Track creator payment history

## Create a job

From the Creator dashboard, select **Create Job** and follow the prompts. Be specific about requirements so contributors can submit valid proof.
