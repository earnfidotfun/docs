## What you can do

The Creator hub is where you manage everything related to jobs you create:

- Create jobs
- Track performance over time (by period)
- Review activity and progress
- Handle disputes and verifications
- Track creator payment history

## Create a job

From the Creator dashboard, select **Create Job** and follow the prompts. Be specific about requirements so contributors can submit valid proof.

For fees, locks, manual review windows, contests, and video settings, read [Creating jobs & platform rules](/creators/creating-jobs).

## Integrations

Two **separate** integration surfaces (pick the one that matches your stack):

- **[Work API](https://app.earnfi.fun/work-api)** — developers: programmatic jobs with Work API keys.  
- **[Agent API](https://app.earnfi.fun/agent-api)** — agents: agentic commerce layer (not an extension of the Work API).
