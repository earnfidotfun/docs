## What you’ll see

The **Verifications** section helps you manage items that need additional review, such as:

- Verification checks tied to job completion
- Appeals where a contributor provides extra proof

## Tips

- Ask for specific missing proof (a direct link, a clearer screenshot, or a timestamped video).
- Be consistent so contributors learn what “good proof” looks like.
