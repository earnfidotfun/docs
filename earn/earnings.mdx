## Earnings overview

In **Earnings & Withdrawals**, you can track balances and history in **USDC on Solana**:

- **Balance**: funds available to withdraw
- **Locked**: rewards that are not yet unlocked
- **Total earned**: your lifetime rewards earned
- **Withdrawn**: completed withdrawals

You can also view your **job completions** and **earnings history**.

## Earnings history labels

Your history lists each credit with a short label, for example:

- **Job reward** — payout from completing a standard job  
- **Contest** — payout from a contest you won  
- **Referral earning** — rewards from your invite network  
- **Bonus rank reward** — extra share from the token-holder bonus pool when a job or contest you took part in finishes (only users in the eligible token ranks for that campaign receive this; amounts are split according to the rules shown in the product)  
- **Unlocked reward**, **Withdrawal refund**, and other occasional line items as they apply to your account  

Open **Earnings** and use the history table or list to page through past activity.

## Earnings card

You can create a **shareable image** of your earnings (16:9, suitable for social posts). Amounts reflect your **real payouts** on EarnFi from unlocked, withdrawn, or contest rewards. The card can include your **referral code** and a **QR code** that links to your personal invite URL.

See [Earnings card](/earn/share-earnings-card) for details and eligibility rules.

## Locked vs. available

Some rewards may be **locked** for a period. As they unlock, they move into your **balance**.

## If you see “Account restricted”

If your account is restricted, earnings and withdrawals may be temporarily unavailable. Contact support at `hello@earnfi.fun` with your wallet address and username.
