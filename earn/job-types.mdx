## What you earn

Job rewards on EarnFi are settled in **USDC on the Solana network**. Your **Earnings** balance and withdrawal flow use that same asset; always double-check the wallet address you provide for withdrawals.

## Categories you’ll see in the Jobs Feed

EarnFi groups work into a few practical categories. The **job card** and **job detail** screens always show what applies to *that* job: reward, slots, deadlines (for contests), lock time, and any proof requirements.

### Social jobs

Social jobs ask you to take an action on a creator’s target (for example follow, like, repost, quote, comment, or a **multi-step** combination).

**Typical flow**

1. Open the job and read the requirements end-to-end.  
2. Use **Open link** / **Copy link** when provided so you land on the correct post or profile.  
3. Complete each required action honestly (multi-step jobs may require every step).  
4. When prompted, pass any **human verification** step if the job requires it.  
5. Submit what the job asks for—often a **verification link**, sometimes **screenshots or video proof**, and sometimes **notes**.  

**Lock period**

Many social jobs show a **lock** duration on the card (for example a number of hours). While rewards are **locked**, they are not yet available in your withdrawable balance. When the lock ends, eligible rewards move according to the job rules shown in the app.

**Quality and spam**

Creators and verification expect **real engagement** that matches the instructions. Low-effort comments, misleading links, or batch copy-paste patterns are likely to be rejected. When screenshots are requested, show **your account** and the **target content** clearly.

### Video view jobs

You need to watch at least the **required watch percentage** of the video to satisfy the job and earn your reward.

- Each video view job has a **minimum watch percentage** that is tracked: if you do not meet the required watch percentage, the job completion fails.  
- When you meet the requirement, submit completion through the **in-app player** (you may need to close the player and tap **COMPLETE JOB**).  
- Rewards for video view jobs are **credited to your balance without a long lock** once verified (see the job card for any exception).  

**Rewards for video view jobs**

EarnFi uses **dynamic pricing** for video view jobs. Creators set the required watch percentage, and the system calculates a **minimum fair reward** from the length of the video and that percentage so pay stays fair. You always see the **reward offered to you** on the job before you start.

### Manual jobs

Manual jobs cover structured tasks that need **human review**—like signups and invites, or more complex work such as content creation and professional services through **Custom job** instructions set by the creator. **Required proofs** are defined by the creator.

**Typical flow**

1. Complete the off-platform steps exactly as written.  
2. Submit a **verification link** when required.  
3. Upload **image and/or video proof** when required (multiple files are supported where the uploader allows).  
4. Add **notes** if the job invites extra context.  

**Review timing**

Your submission goes into the creator’s review queue. Jobs are configured with a **review window**: if the creator does not approve or reject in time, the platform may **finalize the submission automatically** so you are not left waiting indefinitely. Watch **notifications** and **Pending** for status.

**If you are rejected**

You may have a **short dispute window** after a rejection to respond from the dispute flow. Use clear evidence and stay factual.

### Contests

Contests are jobs with **limited participant slots**, a **deadline**, and often a **prize structure** (equal split or tiered prizes).

- Join before slots fill or time runs out.  
- Follow the contest rules for submissions and comments if the host requires them.  
- After the deadline, the contest may enter a **winner selection** phase. Winners and payouts follow the method the creator chose (**manual** or **random**), as shown when the contest was created.

## Proof files: formats and practical limits

### Task proof uploads (submissions)

When the job allows proof uploads, use standard formats the app accepts:

- **Images**: JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP  
- **Video**: MP4, WebM, QuickTime (MOV)  

You can attach **multiple** proofs when the job asks for it. Keep files **reasonably small**—very large uploads may fail on slow connections or hit hosting limits. If an upload fails, **trim**, **compress**, or use **multiple clear screenshots** instead of one huge video.

### Dispute evidence (if you attach files in a dispute)

Dispute attachments are **images only** (JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP), **up to 2 MB per file**. Use crisp screenshots that directly support your case.

## Anti-spam and verification quality (especially social)

- Use **one account** that matches your connected **X** identity when the job depends on X actions.  
- Paste **direct links** to the exact post, comment, or repost—not a generic profile link unless asked.  
- Don’t reuse the same comment across many jobs if the creator expects unique input.  
- If the job says “screenshot required,” don’t skip it—manual review jobs often depend on it.
