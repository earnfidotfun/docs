## What you’ll find

The Jobs Feed is where you discover opportunities to earn. Jobs can include:

- Social tasks (follow/like/comment, etc.)
- Manual verification tasks (submit a link and/or screenshots)
- Contests (limited participants, leaderboard-style rewards)

## Filtering and sorting

Use the controls at the top of the feed to:

- **Refresh**: pull the latest jobs.
- **Filters**: narrow by category/type.
- **Sort**: new first, reward high/low, or progress.

## Open a job

Tap a job card to view details, requirements, and reward. If a job has a target link, you can open it directly or copy it from inside the job view.
