## What “Pending” is

The **Pending** page is your action center for items that may require a response, such as:

- **Disputes** you’re involved in
- **Verification appeals** (when additional review is needed)

## Tabs and counts

Pending is organized into tabs (for example, disputes). Each tab shows a count so you can quickly see what’s waiting.

## Best practices

- Respond before any displayed deadline to avoid delays.
- Keep your proof clear and concise—include links and supporting screenshots when relevant.
