## How referrals work

EarnFi gives you a unique referral link and code. When someone joins using your link and completes jobs, you earn a **commission** on their completed jobs (plus any bonus rewards shown in-app).

## Your referral link and code

On the Referrals page you can:

- Copy your **referral link**
- View your **referral code**
- Track your **referral stats** (total referrals, active referrals, total earned)

## Verification requirements

In some cases, referrals may require **X verification** before you can access the page or use certain features.
