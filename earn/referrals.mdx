## How referrals work

EarnFi gives you a **referral link** and **referral code**. When someone joins through your link and completes eligible jobs, you earn **commission** (and any **bonus** components shown in your stats) on their qualifying activity.

Rewards accrue in **USDC on Solana** like other earnings.

## Your referral dashboard

On the **Referrals** page you can:

- Copy your **referral link**  
- View your **referral code**  
- Track **totals**, **active referrals**, and **lifetime referral earnings**  
- See the split between **standard commission** and **bonus rewards** when both apply  

## Withdrawing referral earnings

**You do not need to connect X** to withdraw **referral earnings**. X is required when you want to **take social jobs** that pay for X actions, but referral payouts follow the normal **Withdraw** rules for USDC on Solana.

## Sharing responsibly

- Explain honestly that rewards depend on **real job completions**, not clicks alone.  
- Do not spam referral links or misrepresent EarnFi’s payouts.
