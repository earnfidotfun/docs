## Earnings card

Show off your earnings with an **EarnFi earnings card**.

## What it is

The **earnings card** is proof of your earnings: a high-resolution image (**1920×1080**, 16:9) you can **download** and **share** on social platforms to celebrate your payouts, grow referrals, and help showcase EarnFi. It highlights real payouts you have already earned on EarnFi.

## What appears on the card

- **Earnings data** — either a **single** unlocked or withdrawn job reward (or a confirmed contest prize), or a **summary** for a time range you pick (for example last 7 days). Numbers reflect **your account on EarnFi**.
- **Your public handle** — shown in a **masked** form (for example a shortened wallet or X handle), not your full private details.
- **Referral code and QR code** — your **personal referral link** is encoded in the QR so others can join with your invite. The same link format works with EarnFi’s normal web addresses (including clean URLs without `#`).

If a reward is not eligible to share, the app will show a clear message instead of generating an image.

## Tips

- Generate the card after rewards show as **unlocked** or **withdrawn** (or contest **winner**) in Earnings.
- If your account is **restricted**, EarnFi may disable sharing actions until the restriction is resolved.
- If generation fails, refresh the page and try again; persistent errors usually mean the reward is not yet eligible for sharing.
