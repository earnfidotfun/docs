## What it is

The **shareable earnings card** is a high-resolution image (**1920×1080**, 16:9) you can download, copy, or post to social platforms. It highlights real payouts you have already earned on EarnFi.

## What appears on the card

- **Earnings data** — either a **single** unlocked or withdrawn job reward (or a confirmed contest prize), or a **summary** for a time range you pick (for example last 7 days). Numbers are taken from **EarnFi’s records** for your account.
- **Your public handle** — shown in a **masked** form (for example a shortened wallet or X handle), not your full private details.
- **Referral code and QR code** — your **personal referral link** is encoded in the QR so others can join with your invite. The same link format works with EarnFi’s normal web addresses (including clean URLs without `#`).

You **cannot** type in your own dollar amounts for the card. If a reward is not eligible to share, the app will show a clear message instead of generating an image.

## Security and fairness (what you should know)

- **Server-side only** — Creating the card calls EarnFi’s API with your signed-in session. The server **recomputes** totals and eligibility from the database; the image is not driven by numbers you paste into the browser.
- **Eligible rewards only** — Single-job cards require **unlocked** or **withdrawn** completions, or **confirmed contest winnings**. Summary cards aggregate the same kinds of completed payouts for the period you select.
- **Referral data** — Your referral code and link on the card match what EarnFi already issued to your account.

## Tips

- Generate the card after rewards show as **unlocked** or **withdrawn** (or contest **winner**) in Earnings.
- If your account is **restricted**, EarnFi may disable sharing actions until the restriction is resolved.
- If generation fails, refresh the page and try again; persistent errors usually mean the reward is not yet eligible for sharing.
