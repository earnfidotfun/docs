## Typical submission flow

1. Open a job from the **Jobs Feed**.
2. Follow the job’s instructions and complete the required actions.
3. When prompted, submit proof of completion.

## Proof types

Depending on the job, EarnFi may ask for one or more of:

- **Verification link** (a URL to your post/comment/transaction/etc.)
- **Screenshot or video proof** (you can upload multiple files)
- **Optional notes** (extra context for the reviewer)

## Tips for getting approved

- Submit proof that clearly shows the required action and your account.
- If the job asks for a link, paste the **exact** URL (not a screenshot of the URL).
- If you upload files, make sure they’re readable (zoomed in if needed).

## After you submit

Once submitted, your job moves into review. If there’s a problem, you may see it reflected in your job status, earnings, or pending actions (disputes/appeals).
