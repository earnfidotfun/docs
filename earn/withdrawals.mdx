## Withdraw your balance

Open **Earnings & Withdrawals** and select **Withdraw** to start a withdrawal from your available balance.

## Withdrawal history

Use **Withdrawal history** to review past withdrawals and their status.

## Verification requirements

In some cases, withdrawals may require **X verification** before you can proceed. If you’re redirected to verification, complete it and return to withdraw.

## If Withdraw is unavailable

Withdraw can be unavailable if:

- Your account is **restricted**
- Your account is **temporarily muted** (until the mute period ends)
