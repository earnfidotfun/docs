## Asset & network

Withdrawals from EarnFi are sent as **USDC on the Solana network**. Make sure the **destination address** you enter is a **Solana wallet** that can receive USDC (typically your linked wallet or another address you control).

## Where to see the rules

Every account follows the **current withdrawal policy** shown directly in the app. Open **Earnings → Withdraw** to see live values for:

- **Minimum** withdrawal amount  
- **Maximum** per request  
- **Auto-processing threshold** (larger amounts may go through additional review with a stated time estimate)  
- **Daily withdrawal count** versus your daily limit  
- **Network / processing notes** (for example timing expectations shown beside the form)  

The Withdraw page is the **source of truth**—policy values can change, so this documentation does not quote fixed numbers.

## Before you withdraw

- Confirm you are withdrawing to the **correct Solana USDC address**.  
- Large requests may show **manual review** messaging with an estimated processing window.  
- If your wallet has never received USDC before, the app may show a **one-time token account creation** cost (displayed in the Withdraw UI) that is deducted from the payout.  

## Withdrawal history

Use **Withdrawal history** to track status for past requests.

## X connection

Connecting **X** is required to **earn from many jobs**, but it is **not** a blocker for **withdrawing referral earnings**. If the app ever asks you to complete an extra step before cashing out, follow the on-screen message—it reflects your account’s current eligibility.

## If Withdraw is unavailable

Withdraw may be disabled when:

- Your account is **restricted**  
- Your account is **temporarily muted** until the mute expires  
- You are below the **minimum** or above the **maximum** for a single request (see the Withdraw page)  
- You have reached your **daily withdrawal count**
