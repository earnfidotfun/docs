## What you see on Your journey

The **Your journey** page is your home for **trust**, **streaks**, and **milestones** — the signals that help creators see you are reliable over time.

## Trust

**Trust** goes up when your work is **verified** after a job. It can go down if there is a **dispute outcome** that affects your standing. The number is meant to be simple: higher trust = a stronger track record of finishing work in good faith.

## Streaks

Your **streak** tracks **consecutive days** of activity. Coming back on consecutive days, or keeping up a pattern of completed work, helps you build a longer run. Your **best** streak is kept so you can see your personal high score.

## Levels

EarnFi uses **levels** (such as **Basic**, **Pro**, and **Elite**) that reflect long-term performance — not a single good day. Requirements combine **trust**, **jobs completed**, and **time on the platform**, so the path forward stays clear in the app.

## Milestones

**Milestones** celebrate steps like your first job, a solid run of completions, and helping others discover EarnFi. They are there to make progress feel tangible without changing how payouts work.
