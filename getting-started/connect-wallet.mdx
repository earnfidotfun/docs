## How sign-in works

EarnFi supports **two ways** to authenticate:

1. **Solana wallet** — Connect with a supported wallet (below). Best when you want a direct on-chain identity and smooth **USDC** flows.  
2. **Google** — **Sign in with Google** for a traditional email-backed session. You can still **connect a wallet** afterward for withdrawals and on-chain actions.

Use whichever fits your setup; you can combine **Google sign-in** with **wallet connection** in the same account flow where the app allows it.

## Supported wallets

EarnFi supports Solana wallets like:

- **Phantom**
- **Solflare**
- **Backpack**

If you don’t have a wallet yet, install one of the options above and come back to connect.

## Sign in with Google

1. Open EarnFi.  
2. Choose **Sign in with Google** (or the Google option on the sign-in screen).  
3. Complete the Google OAuth flow in the popup or redirect.  
4. Return to the app—you’re signed in with your Google-linked EarnFi account.

You can **connect a Solana wallet** later from profile or wallet prompts when you need to receive **USDC**, fund creator jobs, or complete on-chain steps.

## Connect (desktop)

1. Open EarnFi.
2. Click **Connect Wallet**.
3. Select your wallet.
4. Approve the connection inside your wallet.

After connecting, you’ll see your wallet address and you can continue into the app. This wallet is where you’ll receive **USDC on Solana** when you withdraw earnings.

## Connect (mobile)

On mobile, EarnFi can connect via your wallet app when a browser extension isn’t available.

1. Tap **Connect Wallet**.
2. Choose **Connect with Phantom / Solflare / Backpack**.
3. Approve in your wallet app and return to the browser.

## Troubleshooting

- **Wallet shows “Not Installed”**: install the wallet extension (desktop) or use the wallet app connection (mobile).
- **Connection failed**: retry, then confirm you approved the prompt in the wallet.
- **Wrong network warning**: follow the on-screen network guidance in your wallet.
