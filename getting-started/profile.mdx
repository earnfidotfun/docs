## What your profile is used for

Your profile helps job creators identify your work and contact you through the platform experience.

Common places your profile shows up:

- Job submissions
- Disputes and verification flows
- Earnings and activity history

## Recommended setup

- **Choose a consistent username** you’ll use across submissions.
- **Keep your connected wallet the same** when possible to avoid confusion during reviews.
- **Verify when prompted** (for example, X verification may be required for withdrawals/referrals in some cases).
