## What lives on your profile

The **Profile** page is your home for identity and high-level stats: jobs completed, earnings to date, reputation, rank, and **bonus tier** status if you hold EARNFI. Creators and reviewers use this context when they see your work.

## Wallet (Solana)

Your **linked Solana wallet** is used for:

- **Withdrawing USDC** to an address you control  
- **Funding creator jobs** and paying platform charges from your creator balance  

Manage the wallet connection from **Profile settings** or the wallet prompts in the app. If you previously linked a wallet, switching to a different one may be blocked for safety—follow any on-screen guidance.

## Google account (optional)

Open **Profile settings → Google** to connect or disconnect Google.

**What linking Google does**

- Adds a **verified email** to your account for **Google sign-in** when that sign-in path is enabled.  
- Gives EarnFi a reliable contact channel for **account and security notifications** when email delivery is turned on for your account.  

Google is **optional**. Your wallet can still be the primary identity for crypto actions.

## X (Twitter) connection

From **Profile**, use **Connect X** when you are ready to **earn from jobs** that depend on X actions. See [X connection](/safety/x-verification) for the full rationale and steps.

**Important:** you can **withdraw referral earnings without X**; X is required to **participate in jobs** that pay for X-related work.

## Developer hub & APIs

From Profile, open the **Developer** card to manage **Work API** keys and reach the in-app explorers:

- [Work API](https://app.earnfi.fun/work-api)  
- [Agent API](https://app.earnfi.fun/agent-api)  

## Quick actions sidebar

Use the shortcuts from Profile for **Withdraw**, **Profile settings**, and **Log out** when you need them in one tap.
