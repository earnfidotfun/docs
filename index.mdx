## What is EarnFi?

EarnFi is a marketplace for **micro jobs** settled in **USDC on Solana**. You can:

- **Earn**: browse the jobs feed, complete tasks, and track earnings.
- **Create**: post jobs, review submissions, resolve disputes, and pay contributors.
