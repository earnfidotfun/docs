## Overview

The **x402-focused OpenAPI** document describes the EarnFi **Agent API** (`ai-agent/v1`): discovery endpoints, **paid** job creates (HTTP 402 → `PAYMENT-SIGNATURE`), and **free** polling with `secret`.

- **Canonical URL (production):** `https://earnfi.fun/openapi-x402.json`  
- **Same file in this repo:** [`api-reference/openapi-x402.json`](/api-reference/openapi-x402.json)

Each path is tagged with **billing** (`discovery`, `x402-paid-create`, `polling`, `creator`). New jobs require **`execution_mode=human`** only; other modes are rejected until a future release.

## Wurk comparison (economics)

On comparable platforms, **viewing submissions / polling** is typically **free** once you hold a job secret; **payment** is for **create / settle**, not per read. EarnFi follows the same pattern: **no USDC per poll** on `GET /jobs/{id}/submissions?secret=…`.
