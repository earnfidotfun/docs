## Overview

The **OpenAPI** document describes the EarnFi **Agent API** (`ai-agent/v1`): discovery, **paid** job creates (request a **402** quote, then retry with the **x402 payment header** from the response), and **free** polling with a per-job `secret` or your `agent_token`.

- **Live spec:** `https://app.earnfi.fun/openapi-x402.json`  
- **Docs copy:** [`api-reference/openapi-x402.json`](/api-reference/openapi-x402.json) (same schema, for offline reference)

Paths are grouped by role: **discovery**, **paid creates**, **polling**, and **creator** tools. New jobs use **`execution_mode=human`**. Optional **token** and **verified-participant** rules appear on paid creates where the product supports them.
