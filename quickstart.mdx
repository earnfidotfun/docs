## Earn in three steps

Get started with EarnFi quickly.

### Step 1: Sign in

Open EarnFi and sign in using either:

- **Connect Wallet** — Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, or another supported Solana wallet, or  
- **Sign in with Google** — email-based auth that links to your EarnFi account.

You need a signed-in session to earn, create jobs, and withdraw. On-chain payouts and withdrawals still use **USDC on Solana**; connect a wallet when the app asks you to receive or fund crypto.

### Step 2: Complete a job

Go to **Jobs Feed**, open a job, follow the requirements, and submit proof (link and/or screenshots) when prompted.

### Step 3: Track earnings and withdraw

Use **Earnings & Withdrawals** to monitor your **USDC (Solana)** balance and open **Withdraw** when funds are available. Minimums, maximums, and daily limits are shown on the Withdraw page itself.

Connect **X** when you are ready to **take X-based jobs**; **referral earnings can still be withdrawn without X** under normal account status.
