## What a restriction means

If your account is restricted, you can still access EarnFi with limited features.

## What you can still do

- Log in and access the platform
- Create/list jobs or contests (as a creator)
- View job or contest details
- Use **Recover SOL**

## What you can’t do while restricted

- Earn from tasks, jobs, or contests
- Withdraw earnings or access your balance
- Use referrals or referral rewards

## Appeals

If you believe your restriction is a mistake, contact support at `hello@earnfi.fun`.
