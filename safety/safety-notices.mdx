## Security notice (required for jobs)

For many jobs, EarnFi shows a security notice you must acknowledge before continuing.

Key reminders:

- **Never share private keys** or passwords. EarnFi only needs your public wallet address.
- **Verify URLs** and make sure you’re on the official EarnFi site.
- **Use strong security hygiene** (unique passwords, 2FA where possible).
- **Report suspicious activity** if something looks off.

## Why you may see it repeatedly

Some notices are recorded per job session, so you may be asked again for a new job.
