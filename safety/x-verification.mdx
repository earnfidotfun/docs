## When you’ll need X verification

EarnFi may require X verification before you can access:

- Withdrawals
- Withdrawal history
- Referrals

If required, EarnFi will redirect you to **Connect X Account**.

## How it works

1. Open the X verification flow.
2. Follow the on-screen steps to connect your X account.
3. Once connected and approved, you’ll return to the app and can continue.
