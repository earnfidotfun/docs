## Why X matters for earning

Many jobs on EarnFi are tied to real actions on **X (Twitter)**—follows, likes, reposts, quotes, comments, and more. To **participate in jobs and earn rewards**, you need an **X account connected** to your EarnFi profile so submissions can be checked against the right identity.

If you have **not** connected X yet, you can still use EarnFi for other activities that do not require job participation (for example browsing). **Referral earnings remain withdrawable** without an X connection—you can take those rewards out as **USDC on Solana** like any other balance, subject to normal withdrawal rules on the Withdraw page.

## Verified X accounts (blue check and similar)

Some jobs are labeled for **verified X accounts only**. That means the creator chose a stricter audience: EarnFi checks that your linked X profile is **verified** (for example subscription / organization verification) according to our verification service.

### How checks work

- **Server-side** — Verification status is loaded through a **secure service** configured by EarnFi (not something you can spoof from the browser alone).
- **Caching** — Results are **cached** for a period so normal browsing does not hammer external services. Cached data must be **fresh enough** for you to see the job as available to start.
- **At payout** — For verified-only jobs, EarnFi may **check again** before rewards unlock, so losing verification later can affect payout eligibility.
- **Refresh** — You can request a fresh check from the app when prompted. **Forced** refreshes are **rate-limited** to protect the service and keep the app fast for everyone.

If a verified check cannot finish (for example the service is temporarily unavailable), try again later or contact support with your wallet address if it keeps failing.

## How to connect X

1. Open **Profile & Settings** in EarnFi.  
2. In the **Connect X Account** card, choose **Connect X** (or the equivalent button).  
3. Complete the on-screen steps until your account shows as **connected** and **verified** when applicable.  
4. Return to the **Jobs Feed** and open a job—verification steps will recognize your X identity.  

If you ever need to revisit the flow, use **Profile → Connect X** or follow any in-app prompt that appears when you try to start a job that requires X.

## Tips

- Use the **same X account** you will perform actions from.  
- If verification fails, refresh profile status and try again—avoid switching accounts mid-job.  
- Keep your X profile in good standing; unusual or restricted accounts may fail checks.
