## What contests are

Contests are jobs with a limited number of participant slots. Once a contest is full, new participants can’t join until slots are available again.

## Navigating contests

- Open a contest from the **Jobs Feed**
- Use **Back to Jobs** to return to browsing

## Account requirements

Like other jobs, a contest may show an **Account requirements** area: token holder rules (with **name, symbol, and logo** when available), **X** (connected and sometimes **verified** X only), and **minimum rank** if the creator set one. Use **Verify** on the contest page when offered so you know you qualify before you invest time in a submission.

## If a contest is full

If you see a message that the contest is fully completed (all slots filled), browse other contests or check back later.
