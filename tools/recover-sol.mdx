## What Recover SOL does

Recover SOL helps you reclaim SOL rent by:

- Closing **empty** token accounts
- Burning tokens (when needed) and then closing accounts

You’ll see your wallet, recoverable amount, and a history of actions inside the tool.

## Getting started

1. Connect your wallet.
2. Open **Recover SOL** from the sidebar.
3. Choose what you want to do:
   - **Close**: close empty token accounts
   - **Burn**: burn tokens (if required) and close

## Tips

- If you see “No empty accounts”, switch to **Burn** to handle accounts that still have a balance.
- If your wallet is disconnected, use the in-page **Connect Wallet** prompt.
